How it works

Script to professional video
in minutes.

1
Write or upload your script
Paste text, write inside the editor, or upload a TXT, DOCX, or PDF. Your words, your voice.
2
AI segments into scenes
Smart scene segmentation splits your script at natural speech boundaries based on your speaking speed and scene duration.
3
Plan your scene board
Assign images, charts, and videos to each scene. Reorder, merge, or split scenes. Add notes.
4
Record with teleprompter OR use AI presenter
Professional teleprompter with adaptive scroll. Or generate an AI presenter avatar from your script.
5
Edit scene by scene
Trim, replace, or re-record individual scenes. Add background music, adjust audio, arrange timeline.
6
Export for every platform
16:9, 9:16, 1:1, or 4:5. Optimised presets for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, and Facebook.
